Online Complaints System

We support an online complaints system for the students to hear students' voices. Your complaints will be managed by the responsible person. You can freely report your complaints here.

Belief
We believe our students' strong and effective complaints can help to improve quality of services and administration.

You can communicate by phone, email and contact us using this system. You can discuss any difficulties in our university with us.
Can we complaints the system?
Of course, you can complaint about any difficulties of using this system. This system is a part of the university and your complaints can make better changes to this system.
